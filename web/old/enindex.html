XVI Foro Pensando en Colombia

"A thought made by the youth for Colombia"

About Us

The XVI Foro Pensando en Colombia is a space created by young people for Colombia, where the problems that affect the country are made known and which through debates, presentations and talks, possible solutions will be reached. The intention of these spaces is to develop critical, respectful and inclusive thinking of each of the attendees, acknowledging the opinion and perception of others. On the other hand, we seek to encourage participants to prepare through this space to be the future generation that will fill Colombia with hope and adaptability. The PEC Forum seeks the participation of all students in the country and overseas to achieve an event where we all have as our main objective, to make Colombia a better country.

How To Participate

  • The registrations of the students of High and Middle Schools students from the invited schools will be sent through the form located on this webpage (registration button).

  • The presentations that will take place will need to take into account the list of suggested topics or any other that is similar.

  • The presentations will have to be previously studied for approval according to the forum schedule.

  • * In due course a separate form will be enabled in which a member of each presentation must register their presentation.
