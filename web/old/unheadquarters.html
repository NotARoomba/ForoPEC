UN Headquarters

Introducing for the first time ever in a Foro Pensando en Colombia edition, the United Nations Headquarters committee will host a global and systemic debate space regarding compromising geopolitical current conflicts that affect us as society. The delegates will diplomatically argue in order to reach the greater good for the international community, writing working papers that will furtherly be voted.
